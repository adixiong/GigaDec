Who We Are

We are a team of developers and former Arizona Decathletes passionate about both exploring the possibilities unlocked by AI and helping current Decathletes through their competitive journey.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing consistent, measurable improvements in the performance of our client teams and competitors, in all classes - Honors, Scholastics, and Varsities alike. With the help of AI, we strive to optimize the studying experience for current and future Decathletes, and create interest for future students to participate in programs like AcaDec and Speech & Debate.
